Piṇḍoddāna: Contents of the Vastu. This Vastu describes the the institutionalization varṣāvāsa, the stay in a monastery during the rainy season, with the Buddha providing the rules for it. Background: When the disciples of the Buddha were wandering around during the rainy season, the Tīrthikas said that the Śākya monks did not even have a place to stay during the rainy season, and this was the reason why the disciples asked the Buddha to introduce the varṣāvāsa. And the Buddha made the rules. Panglung p. 14.
